Chicken Noodle Soup

Description

This is a Chicken Noodle Soup it is great for cold nights and especially great for when you are sick in bed

as a disclaimer it is not Vegan or keto friendly!

Ingredients

  • Chicken stock
  • shredded Chicken
  • small noodles of your choice
  • carrots
  • celery
  • parsley

Steps

  1. heat up the Chicken stock on the stove on medium heat
  2. add the carrots and celery into the pod
  3. taste the pot after the celery and carrots have had time to cook thoroughly, add salt and pepper as needed
  4. include the shredded chicken into the pot now
  5. add parsley into the pot to taste
