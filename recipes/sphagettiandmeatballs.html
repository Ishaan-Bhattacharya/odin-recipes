Sphagetti and meatballs

Description

This is a great italian dinner for the whole familly

as a disclaimer it is not Vegan or keto friendly!

Ingredients

  • Ragu marinara sauce
  • Sphagetti
  • ground beef
  • onions
  • breadcrumbs
  • egg

Steps

  1. add a little oil and onions to a pot until they get clear
  2. form ground beef into tiny balls
  3. add an egg and breadcrumbs to the tiny balls to make them a bit larger
  4. boil a pot of water
  5. insert Sphagetti into the pot of water and follow instructions on the package for cooking the Sphagetti
  6. place the meatballs on the bottom of the onion pot such that they get some texture
  7. turn the meatballs ocassionaly
  8. take the ragu from the package and add it into the pot of meatballs
  9. when Sphagetti is done, take some of the Sphagetti and some of the water and place it into the pot with the meatballs
  10. serve to your liking and ENJOY!
