Ham Sandwich

Description

This is a hamandcheese Sandwich it is very easy to make and great lunch to take to school if you fill it with enough protein.

as a disclaimer it is not Vegan or keto friendly!

Ingredients

  • White Bread
  • Mayonnaise
  • Jar of pickles
  • sliced deli ham
  • sliced american cheese
  • mustard

Steps

  1. lay down the piece of bread
  2. add the mustard and mayo to a side of the bread
  3. place the sliced deli ham on the mustard and mayo that is on top of the bread
  4. take some pickles out the jar and place the on the Sandwich
  5. place a single piece of american cheese on pickles that is above the ham
  6. close the sandwhich and ENJOY!
